Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with us.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A typical web application takes 3-4 weeks from discovery to launch. We break projects into sprints and provide regular progress updates. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements.

Do you work with startups or only established businesses?

We work with businesses of all sizes, from startups to large enterprises. For startups, we offer flexible engagement models and can help with product strategy, MVP development, and scaling. We believe in the potential of African startups and offer competitive pricing for early-stage companies.

What is your pricing model?

We offer several pricing models to fit different needs: fixed-price projects for well-defined scopes, time-and-materials for evolving projects, and dedicated team models for long-term partnerships. During our discovery phase, we'll recommend the best approach for your project and provide transparent pricing.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-launch support packages including maintenance, updates, hosting management, and technical support. We believe in building long-term partnerships with our clients and ensure your digital solution continues to perform optimally after launch.

How do you ensure project security and confidentiality?

We take security seriously. All team members sign NDAs, we use secure development practices, implement role-based access controls, and can work with your security team to meet specific compliance requirements. We're also happy to sign additional confidentiality agreements to protect your intellectual property.
